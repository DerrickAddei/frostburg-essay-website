The Challenge We Face

The World Is Getting Darker

It seems to be the case that every week we hear of another human tragedy. The hate in this world is thickening and spreading like a virus. Brethren, now is not a time for silence.

1 in 3 Students

A recent study by the Foundation for Individual Rights and Expression (FIRE) showed that 1 in 3 students now believe violence is a justifiable means to stop someone from expressing their opinion.

(Weiss and Lenthall-Cleary)

Our Answer

Our Response is the Love of Christ

Everyone from the world to America to Frostburg is asking, “Where do we go next?” Well let’s tell them, because we have an answer. The answer is Christ.

"Love is patient, love is kind, it is not jealous; love does not brag, it is not arrogant... it does not rejoice in unrighteousness, but rejoices with the truth..." - 1 Corinthians 13:4-7
Our Method

We Don't Fight People, We Fight Ideas

The world fights battles by cheating, deception, and cruelty, but this is not how we fight. Our weapons are not of the flesh.

Listen & Empathize

We listen and are patient. We empathize and show compassion. We stand firm but not with pride.

Live as Examples

We teach, we correct, we guide. This fight demands proactiveness; it is not simply enough to pray.

Go & Make Disciples

God waits for us to act. He has given us the power: "Go, therefore, and make disciples of all the nations." (Matt. 28:19)

One Person Can't Wrap Their Arms Around the Earth.

But eight billion can. We don't have to reach everyone. We just have to reach our neighbor. If I can't reach people in Europe, at least I can reach my neighbor, you.
